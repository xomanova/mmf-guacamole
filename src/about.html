About

This site is a hobby project, created so that my friends and I can play our favourite games no matter where we are. It's grown since to provide the same for thousands around the world. It brings me joy to know that I've enabled a little bit of fun in the lives of so many.

If you need any help or have any suggestions for the site, head over to the Help Centre to file a ticket, or email me at support@netgames.io.

I want to make sure that presenting these games here is okay with the original publishers. If you are a publisher trying to reach out, you can contact me at admin@netgames.io.

I spend a lot of time and effort keeping netgames.io going, and so long as people keep using it I'll keep it up. If you'd like to say thanks, I'd really appreciate a coffee :)
