      Gameplay

      Each person has one card in their hand. Players take turns drawing one card and discarding one card.

      When you voluntarily "discard" a card, you perform the action written upon it. All discarded cards are displayed at the top of the screen.

      The player left with the highest card at the end
      wins the round.

      In a player game, the first player to win
      rounds wins the game.

      Other details

      A random card is removed from the deck at the start of each round.

      For two player games, 3 additional cards are discarded face up each round.

      If a round is drawn, the player who discarded the highest total value of cards wins the round.
